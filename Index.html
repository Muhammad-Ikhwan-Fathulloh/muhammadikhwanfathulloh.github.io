About Me

My name is Muhammad Ikhwan Fathulloh. Originally from Indonesia, born in Jakarta. Now living in Bandung, becoming a student at a private university called the Bandung College of Technology with a major in Informatics Engineering. Here I am active in the OXIGEN Student Activity Unit which focuses on technology.

As well as taking part in off-campus activities that focus on the art of paper folding or Origami at Maya Hirai of Origami School and the Indonesian Origami Studio to channel my artistic hobby.

An engineering student who has a hobby of art. Able to think of an idea from environmental observations and abstract it in visual imagination, to assess whether it is in accordance with the surrounding problem which can be used as a real solution.

Loves to work with a team to produce results that solve problems so find the right solution and fast. Have good public speaking skills and are able to lead a team to solve problems and find solutions. Easy to adapt to technological developments and the surrounding environment.
